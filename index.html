FAQ

Plain answers before you submit.

Is the free score really free?

Yes. No credit card, no sales call, no obligation. The free scan is a short public-data report delivered by email. You get the score, top 3 leaks, and one quick fix — whether or not you ever buy anything.

Do you need access to my accounts?

No. The free scan uses only publicly visible data — your website, Google Business Profile, and AI search results. The full audit adds manual verification using the same public signals, plus screenshots. We never ask for login credentials.

What happens after I submit?

You submit your business name, website, city, category, and email — takes about 60 seconds. We manually check your public signals within 24 hours and email your score. The report includes your leak score, estimated leak range with the math shown, top 3 leaks, and one quick fix per leak. No one will call you. No follow-up pitch. If you want to go deeper, just reply to the email.

Who reviews my scan?

Every Revenue Leak Score is built and reviewed by Kenny Meyers — not outsourced, not auto-generated. If a signal can't be confirmed from public data, it doesn't go in the report. Every report passes QA before sending. Reply to your scan email and it goes directly to Kenny — no support queue.

How are the estimated leak ranges calculated?

We use a conservative formula: estimated missed leads per month × your average job value × estimated close rate. The formula is shown in your report so you can check the math. These are estimates based on visible gaps, not guarantees of lost or recovered revenue.

What if my score comes back strong?

A good score is useful too. It tells you which systems are already working so you can stop worrying about them. Most businesses score between 35 and 70. If yours is strong, the scan confirms that — which also matters.

Will someone call me after I submit?

No. This is email-only. Your scan arrives in your inbox within 24 hours. If you want to go deeper, reply to the email. No one will call you unless you specifically ask.

How is this different from an SEO or marketing audit?

A standard SEO audit focuses on traffic, keywords, and rankings. Revenue Leak Score focuses on where traffic that already arrives may fail to convert — missed calls, slow follow-up, weak mobile CTAs, stale reviews, and AI search gaps. Different problem, different lens. No retainer pitch at the end — the $299 full audit is a one-time flat fee.

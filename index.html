FAQ

Plain answers before you submit.

Is the free score really free?

Yes. No credit card, no sales call, no obligation. The free scan is a short public-data report delivered by email. You get the score, top 3 leaks, and one quick fix — whether or not you ever buy anything.

Do you need access to my accounts?

No. The free scan uses only publicly visible data — your website, Google Business Profile, and AI search results. The full audit adds manual verification using the same public signals, plus screenshots.

How are the estimated leak ranges calculated?

We use a conservative formula: estimated missed leads per month × your average job value × estimated close rate. The formula is shown in the report so you can check the math yourself. These are estimates based on visible gaps, not guarantees of lost or recovered revenue.

What if my score comes back strong?

A good score is valuable too — it tells you what's already working so you can stop worrying about it. Most businesses score between 35 and 70. A strong score just means the audit will confirm that, which is also useful to know.

Will someone call me after I submit?

No. This is email-only. Your free scan arrives in your inbox within 24 hours. If you want to follow up, you can reply to the email. No one will cold-call you.

How is this different from an SEO audit?

A standard SEO audit focuses on traffic, keywords, and rankings. Revenue Leak Score focuses on where traffic that already exists may be failing to convert — missed calls, slow follow-up, weak CTAs, stale reviews, and AI search gaps. Different problem, different lens.
